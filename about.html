About us:

Our club is a collaboration of students with their teacher to invent creations in the field of robotics and programming using Arduino and it is a popular open-source electronic prototyping platform. It consists of a microcontroller and a software development environment that allows enthusiasts , educators, students, and professionals to create interactive electronic projects and programmable devices.

The goals of our club :

Our programming and robotics club aims to provide an engaging and educational environment for our students. We have several key goals, including fostering a deep passion for technology and innovation among our members, whether they are beginners or experienced programmers. We encourage the development of coding skills, critical problem-solving abilities, and a strong understanding of robotics principles. Additionally, we strive to promote teamwork and collaboration by working on exciting projects, participating in robotics competitions , and organizing coding challenges. We also aim to extend our impact to the wider community by sharing our knowledge through workshops, outreach programs, and educational initiatives. Ultimately, our club seeks to prepare our members for future STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) opportunities and careers while fostering a love for learning and exploration in the fields of programming and robotics.
